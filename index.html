About Chandra Arun Crop Science

It was in 1940’s when Mr Chandrika Prasad took the initiative of starting a seed business that would reach the masses. The initiative took its modern shape in 1980, when one of his sons, Mr Arun Chandra joined the business in 1980. Through his consistent efforts and hard work he developed one of the most popular varieties of Long Melon (Kakri) Tar Super Chandra Prabha. He has a massive experience in the seed industry and now a new firm is established under his valuable guidance and supported by the name Chandra Arun Crop Science. Now his legacy is being carried forward in a more scientific, innovative and professional manner by Saurabh Chandra- MSC (Ag) ( Genetic and Plant Breeding) MBA ( Agribusiness) ( Bronze Medalist) and Gaurav Chandra (MBA- International Business).

  • Plants are dwarf and beeshy, height 40-50 cm, branches 4-5 per plant.

  • The cultivar is resistant to golden mosaic virus and gives green pod yield of about 125-150 Q/ha.

Scope of seeds in India

Agriculture is the science which deals with management of field crops and animals. The field crops mean any plant which supports the life of human in way of food, cloth, shelter, fuel and raw material for industries.

The Organization was started with "Need based Research " in the field of seed technology for farming community. The mandate includes crop improvement in respect of hybrids and varieties development.
